Overview

Unified multimodal understanding and physically grounded motion generation for humanoid robots.

OmniTrack encodes language, audio, trajectory, and motion references into a shared discrete space, then decodes streaming motion tokens into continuous robot joint controls. This pipeline balances fast response and motion stability, enabling open-world interaction and online reference tracking.
